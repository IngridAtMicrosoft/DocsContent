# Open a jar

1. Place the jar in the palm of your non-dominant hand.
2. With your dominant hand, unscrew the lid.
3. Place the lid on the working surface.
4. With your dominant hand, peel the seal away from the top of the jar.
5. Place the seal on the working surface.
6. Set the jar on the working surface.
